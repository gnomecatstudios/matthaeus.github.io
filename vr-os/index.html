VR-OS

A new operating system beyond the limits of your monitor

VR-OS is an operating system that replaces the monitor with a head-mounted display for a revolutionary desktop computing experience. Use mouse, keyboard, and all the apps you love. Right from your desk, in an immersive environment that allows powerful workflows like never before.






  • All-day compatible

    Use your mouse, trackpad, keyboard. Sit at your desk or on a sofa. VR-OS is based on the idea that using VR should be as comfortable as any desktop or laptop. Even when used many hours at a time. The experience is carefully designed to allow for as much control as possible with the minimum effort. No moving around your furniture, no getting up required.

  • All the space you want

    Because VR-OS makes use of a head-mounted display instead of a monitor, there are no limitations to how much content you will be able to use at the same time.

  • All the apps you love

    VR-OS does not require apps to be updated specifically for 3D or immersive use cases. Any app you love from your desktop today will run equally well here.

  • Seamless collaboration

    Invite any friend or co-worker into your space and collaborate seamlessly. Pass around windows, edit each other's content. Never before has it been possible to share a workflow this easily.

  • Familiar input devices, reinvented

    Keep using mouse and keyboard to comfortably interact with all your apps. But make use of unique features, such as custom keyboard layouts, augmented input assistance, visual hotkeys, and many more.

  • Games and movies on the big screen

    Get immersed in your favorite games and movies, even if they are just made for 2D screens. Blow them up as big as you want, but donâ€™t lose track of your other apps at the same time.

  • New types of windows

    Windows in VR-OS are updated to make the most out of the space. Compare multiple browser tabs at a glance, group windows to move them together, magnify or shrink them at will.

  • Full scale 3D

    Explore and edit 3D content at natural scale, as if you were there, comfortably from your desk. Combine 3D and 2D content for flexible workflows without taking off the headset.
